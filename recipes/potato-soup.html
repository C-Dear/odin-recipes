Potato Soup

Description:

This loaded potato soup recipe is simple, easy, and only needs one pot. It should take no longer than thirty minutes to prepare.

Ingredients:

  • six strips of uncooked, diced bacon
  • 3 tbsp. of butter, salted or unsalted
  • 1 chopped medium onion
  • 3 large minced garlic cloves
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 lbs. peeled, diced gold potatoes
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 tsp. of salt
  • 1 tsp. of ground pepper
  • 2/3 cup of sour cream
  • Shredded cheddar cheese, chives, additional sour cream and bacon for topping

Cooking Steps:

  1. Place bacon strips into soup pot over medium heat. Cook until bacon is crisp and browned.
  2. Remove bacon pieces and set aside, leave fat in pot.
  3. Add butter and chopped onion, cook over medium heat until onions are tender.
  4. Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  5. Sprinkle the flour over the ingredients in the pot and stir until smooth.
  6. Add diced potatoes to the pot along with chicken broth, milk, heavy cream, salt, pepper, and chili powder. Stir well.
  7. Bring to a boil and cook until potatoes are tender when pierced with a fork.
  8. Reduce heat to simmer and remove approximately half of the soup to a blender. Puree until smooth.
  9. Return the pureed soup to the pot, add sour cream and reserved bacon pieces. Stir well.
  10. Allow soup to simmer for 15 minutes before serving.
  11. Top with additional sour cream, bacon, cheddar cheese, or chives.
