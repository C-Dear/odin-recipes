Delicious Gyro

Description:

This simple dish dates back to ancient greece, and is very tasty for how little work needs to be invested.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. ground lamb or beef
  • 2 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1 tsp. black pepper
  • 1 1/2 tsp. ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp. dried oregano
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup minced red onion
  • 2 tsp. freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Oil applied as needed
  • 4 rounds flatbread or pita
  • 4 leaves of lettuce
  • 1 medium tomato, sliced or diced
  • 1/2 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Tzatziki sauce

Cooking Steps:

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Create meat mixture by combining the ground meat with salt, black pepper, cumin, nutmeg, oregano, garlic, red onion, and lemon juice inside a mixing bowl.
  3. Divide the mixture into founr equal portions and shape into oblong patties about three inches wide by siz inches long, and a half of an inch thick. The oblong shape helps it fit inside the flatbread. Refrigerate the patties for one hour.
  4. Preheat a grill or grill pan over high heat. You can also use a skillet.
  5. If grilling, brush the grill or pan with oil. If you are instead pan-frying, add just a dollop of oil into the pan. Grill or fry the patties for three or four minutes per side, or until cooked through with crispy edges.
  6. Spread Tzatziki sauce down in the center of a flatbread round or the pocked of the pita.
  7. Add a lettuce leaf, some sliced tomato, and a few thin onion slices to the bread.
  8. Add the patty and fold the bread around all the ingredients. Repeat with the remaining sandwiches. Serve and enjoy.
